Can I Create ECSs of Different Flavors on a DeH?
================================================

Yes. You can create ECSs of different type and flavors on a DeH.

For example, you can create c4 ECSs on c4 DeHs. c4 includes a series of flavors, such as c4.large.2 and c4.xlarge.2.
